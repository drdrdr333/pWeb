WELCOME TO THE PLAYER SELECTION SITE

Hello, and welcome to the player selection site.
We are so happy you have decided to join us!

The purpose of this site is to allow you to pick baseball pitchers according to the type of numbers you would allow them to have.

You have the option of selecting 3 from the following 5 categories including the following:
  • ERA - meaning earned runs average
  • IP - meaning total innings pitched
  • SO/9 - meaning average strikeouts per 9 innings pitched
  • BB/9 - meaning average walks per 9 innings pitched
  • WHIP - walks + hits per inning pitched - an average of how many runners you allow per inning pitched
